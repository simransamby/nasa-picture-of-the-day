NASA Open Source Development

NASA continues to expand opportunities for the public to interact and engage with its missions and initiatives. NASA is a leader in the use of challenges and prize competitions within the federal government. We believe in the power of open innovation, specifically through incentive prizes, challenges, crowd-sourcing, and citizen science, to help address those problems in partnership with innovators from around the country and the globe. We seek to get the public involved in their space program and are creating opportunities regularly for broad engagement through our prizes, challenges and crowd-sourcing activities.
